Redact PHI and PII from Apache Kafka data

Phirestream redacts PHI and PII from Apache Kafka data streams. With Phirestream you can keep sensitive information out of your data pipelines.

Phirestream runs entirely in your own cloud. Your data never leaves your network.

Quick Start | User's Guide

Works with Apache Kafka

Phirestream can redact PHI and PII as it moves through your Apache Kafka streams, helping to keep sensitive information out of your Kafka topics.

Phirestream runs in your cloud

Phirestream runs in your cloud for optimal data security. Your data never leaves your cloud. It's your data - keep it away from third parties.
